About the Studio

Manus Webworks exists to design and maintain digital systems that are durable, understandable, and useful. The studio operates as an umbrella for independent projects, experiments, and applications rather than as a single commercial product.

About the Designer

Manus Webworks was founded and designed by John Hand, known online as TheQuantifier. His work focuses on building clear, principled digital systems that balance structure, usability, and long-term thinking.

Drawing from interests in mathematics, software engineering, and philosophy, he approaches digital tools as systems to be understood, refined, and trusted rather than simply used.
